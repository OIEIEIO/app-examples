Your Example App

If you are selling complex parametric products, it might be interesting for you or your business to make a custom 3D product configurator website.

This is an example application which demonstrates possibilities to integrate Bit by bit developers platform in your own websites, configurators or webshops. The idea is to use iframe of your public project on bitbybit.dev and communicate with it through special iframe messaging protocol that we expose in bitbybit.dev API.

By downloading STEP file you can easily 3D print it. To understand how to do that and learn how the script works you can enroll in this free course on our school at school.bitbybit.dev
